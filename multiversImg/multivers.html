What is multivers and self-varient?


As we know, the name of our universe is 'The Cosmos,' which contains our Milky Way galaxy. According to the multiverse theory, there is a possibility that many other billions of universes with different laws of nature can coexist with ours. This collection of universes is referred to as the 'Multiverse.' In the multiverse, every possibility that you could think of gives birth to a new universe.


It is possible that in another universe, there is a variant of yourself. It's like another version of you who can be quite similar to you in looks, personalities, and many other things. There is a very popular speculative concept that inside our universe, what we dream is actually happening with a variant of ours in another universe. So that means every dream you see could be true in another universe. Even if you see any unrealistic thing in your dream, like a cartoon, that means it's also true and present in a universe.
